About ExpiryX

Designed to feel calm, premium, and dependable—like your digital document wallet.

ExpiryX helps you stay ahead of expiries by keeping invoices and warranty cards organized in a clean, lightweight format. Add documents in seconds, let the app auto-fill details, and rely on timely reminders so you never miss a warranty, subscription renewal, or product expiry again.

Developer story

The people behind ExpiryX—and why it’s built to be reliable.

Ramesh Kumar Project Manager at NIC (Government sector) • 12+ years in Java & full‑stack development
12+ years building dependable systems

ExpiryX started with a simple frustration: important invoices and warranty cards are easy to misplace, and expiry dates are easy to forget—until it’s too late. Over the years, Ramesh has worked across Java platforms and full‑stack systems where reliability, auditability, and user trust aren’t optional—they’re the baseline.

That mindset shaped ExpiryX into a calm, focused experience: capture a document in seconds, extract the key details, and let reminders quietly do their job. The goal is not to overwhelm you with features, but to make one thing feel effortless—staying ahead of expiries with clean data and a premium, dependable workflow.

Today, while working as a Project Manager at NIC in the government sector, Ramesh continues to bring disciplined engineering practices to the product—designing flows that are simple on the surface and robust underneath.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

What can I store in ExpiryX?
Store invoices, warranty cards, and any item where an expiry date matters—products, memberships, and subscriptions.
Why only two photos per document?
It keeps documents fast to load and easy to manage. Most use-cases are covered by invoice + warranty card.
How do reminders work?
After you save a document with an expiry date, ExpiryX schedules reminders ahead of time so you can act early.
Is sign-in secure?
Yes—ExpiryX supports OTP-based sign-in for quick and secure access.
